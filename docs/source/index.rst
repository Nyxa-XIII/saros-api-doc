Welcome to the Saros docs!
==========================

**Saros** (/seh'raas/) is a discord bot created by Asami Umbra to enhance the over-all productivity of the husks of underworld headquarters.

Saros is capable of assisting with tasklist management, translating to 31 languages, creating and executing reminders, and more!

Check out the :doc:`usage` section for further information, including how to meet and get started working with him!

.. note::

   This project is under active development.
